About us WHAT IS VedyA?

VedyA is a Moroccan inspired boutique hostel located in a tiny village about a 5 minute scooter ride away from the beach. We offer private and dorm style accommodation, a vegan restaurant, surf instruction, and yoga and ayurveda retreats.
